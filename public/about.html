About EVS Small Engine Repair

With over two decades of dedicated service to the Howard County community, Ernest has built a reputation grounded in reliability, precision, and local trust. We understand that your outdoor power equipment is an investment in your property’s beauty and upkeep, which is why we treat every mower, blower, and chainsaw as if it were our own.

Our deep-rooted expertise ensures that whether you are a weekend gardener or a professional landscaper, you receive the seasoned advice and technical skill necessary to tackle any job with confidence. We specialize in high-performance maintenance and repair, focusing on keeping your equipment running like new long after it leaves the showroom floor. By combining traditional craftsmanship with modern diagnostic techniques, we extend the lifespan of your tools and optimize their efficiency. At Ernest, we don’t just fix engines; we provide the peace of mind that comes from knowing your gear is ready to perform whenever duty calls.
